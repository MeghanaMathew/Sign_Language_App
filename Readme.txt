American Sign Language (ASL) is a sign language that has more than serves about 250,000 to 500,000 persons mostly from Deaf communities in the United States. Despite being the only medium of expression used by the people with impaired hearing, most schools do not include ASL in their curriculum. As a result, many people lack the skills required to communicate effectively with from the deaf community. 

This project aims to develop an android application that can help people learn and practise some common words in American Sign Language.
